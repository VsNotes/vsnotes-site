Frequently asked questions

Do I need to hit Save?

No — VSNotes saves your content automatically across tabs, the navigation tree, recordings, and more.

What types of content can I store?

Formatted text, images, tables, files, and links. JSON data gets pretty‑printed with a tree view. Screenshots and recordings are organized for quick access.

Where do my recordings go?

Recordings are auto‑saved into the Recorder folder in the left navigation. Pause/resume and camera on/off are supported.

Can I open websites inside VSNotes?

Yes. Open links like Jira, Google, YouTube, Office online, and more directly in the center panel — no external browser needed.

Does the spreadsheet support long text?

Yes. Double‑click a cell with long data to edit it in a separate view for better visibility. You can style cells, merge, and color them.

How does the meeting bar work?

If Outlook is available, VSNotes shows upcoming meetings at the bottom of the screen so you don’t miss anything.
